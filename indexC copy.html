WELCOM TO THE ST. GEORGE'S BUSINESS CLUB

Hello and welcome to the St.George's Business Club website. In this club, the main goal is to provide opportunities for students to utilize their business skills. We will provide opportunities to compete in business competitions. In addition, we will have guest speakers coming in to teach these students on how to win these competitions. To find out more, please go our 'About' page. If you have any questions, please to do not hesitate to contact any of us!! (Contact information can be found in the 'Our Team' page)
